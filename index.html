HOI AN TRAVEL TOUR GUIDE

3.Hoi An Countryside Sightseeing by Bike & Buffalo Riding & Bamboo Basket Boat with Meal (price : 700.000VND)

Picking up time :
8.25 am to 12.30pm (morning tour)
1.25 pm to 5.30pm (afternoon tour)
This trip for those who love to discover rural life, want to immerse themselves into the atmosphere of peaceful, rustic landscapes and get closer to the nature, friendly people in the Vietnam countryside

Details:

Pickup

Our guides meet you up at the Meeting Point (Godiva - Dinks & Cakes at 11 Hoang Dieu Street, Hoi An )
Travel to eco village by bike . Contemplate the beauty of the countryside in Vietnam, have photo opportunities of the rice paddies, and sumptuous landscapes, and sightseeing some special animals on the Vietnam rice fields. Then, having chance to ride the buffalo and feed them grass. The feeling when you are sitting on the buffalo’s back is amazing.

Action

Move on to the water coconut forest. Ride through the coconut palm jungle and have a chance to row basket boats with the locals. Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the Nipa Palm Forest and enjoy some local activities on the river.
Enjoy local food for lunch/dinner at a restaurant:Hoi An fried spring rolls, banana flower salad, stir fried water morning glory with rice; Steamed red snapper and seasonal fruits for dessert.
Finish & back to the Meeting Point.

This is our menu (take it for reference)

Hoi An fried spring rolls
Banana flower salad
Stir fried water morning glory with rice
Seasonal fruits for dessert
Note: Please advise any specific dietary requirements at time of booking and the menu sometimes could be changed depending on reality conditions
